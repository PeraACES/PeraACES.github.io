Project Fund




The major objective of this fund is to support individual and group projects of ACES members. The fund is donated by ACES Alumni and managed by a panel of staff members (including the head of the department and the senior treasurer of ACES) and the executive committee of ACES. This fund is maintained separately by ACES and it has no connections to the regular funds generated and spent by ACES.

Those who are working on projects (typically hardware projects where you need additional funding) such as the final year project, and project for competitions will write a proposal with a BoM and request for support from the ACES Project Fund. The panel will meet and evaluate such projects and if found appropriate, will cater the request. The projects funded will become part of the department property.

It is worth noting that the project fund is used in situations where the University fund is hard to come by for various reasons including the financial regulations. For instance, buying a hardware component such as a sensor online has a number of limitations, if we are to use the University funds.

In the short period of time of starting this fund, a number of attractive activities were sponsored by the fund and in our situation, the fund was utilized to win a place in a robotics competition organized by a private higher educational institute.

In the second case, it was used to support a large number of projects under a course titled Embedded Systems Design and an exhibition was held inviting industry and academic to witness the event.
